Frequently Asked Questions

Question: How long does a typical river rafting trip last?
Answer: We offer rafting trips that range from 1 to 5 days, you can see the option on the booking page


Question: Are there different difficulty levels for river rafting?
Answer: Yes! There are 6 levels of rafting waters called "classes" More details about classes are available on the booking page.


Question: How many people can go on a rafting trip together?
Answer: 28 is our limit for one trip; four rafts, seven people per raft


Question: What happens in case of bad weather or river conditions?
Answer: We will attempt to reschedule the trip, if that isn't an option we will offer a refund.
